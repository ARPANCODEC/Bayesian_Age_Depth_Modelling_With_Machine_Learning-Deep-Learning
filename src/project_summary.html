🔬 Step 1: Laboratory Data Collection

1

Sediment cores are collected from field sites and analyzed in the laboratory to obtain:

  • Depth (cm) - Physical depth in the sediment core
  • Age (years) - Radiocarbon or other dating methods
  • Error (± years) - Analytical uncertainty in dating
  • Lab ID - Unique identifier for each sample

📊 Step 2: RBacon Age-Depth Modeling

2

RBacon (Bayesian Age-Depth Modeling) processes the raw data to generate:

  • Mean Age - Average predicted age at each depth
  • Median Age - Median predicted age
  • 95% Confidence Intervals - Lower and upper bounds (lo95, hi95)
  • CI Width - Uncertainty range (hi95 - lo95)
  • Accumulation Rate - Sediment accumulation rate (%)
  • Sedimentation Rate - Rate of sediment deposition (cm/year)

💡 RBacon Output Example

For a sample at depth 143cm with age 1120±30 years, RBacon produces:
Mean Age: 2,626 years | 95% CI: 1,990 - 4,095 years
Accumulation Rate: 23.8% | Sedimentation Rate: 0.22 cm/year

🎯 How the Model Makes Predictions

1
Input Processing: New sediment data (depth, age, error) is scaled using the same StandardScaler from training.
2
Forward Pass: Scaled inputs pass through 4 hidden layers with ReLU activation, batch normalization, and dropout.
3
Output Generation: The output layer produces 7 values (mean_age, median_age, lo95, hi95, ci_width, acc_rate, sed_rate).
4
Inverse Scaling: Predictions are transformed back to original units using the output scaler.
5
Validation: Automated checks ensure confidence intervals are valid (Hi95 > Lo95) and ages are non-negative.

🔑 Key Findings & Insights

✓
Age predictions are extremely accurate (R² > 0.99) - The model excels at predicting mean age, median age, and confidence intervals.
⚠️
Accumulation rate predictions need improvement (R² = 0.58) - More training data or different features may be needed.
📊
Sedimentation rate predictions are good (R² = 0.85) - Usable for most applications with caution.
⚠️
Model struggles with very deep/old samples - Negative age predictions occur for depths beyond training range.

💡 Recommendations

✅ Production Ready

Age predictions (mean_age, median_age, lo95, hi95, ci_width) are production-ready with >99% accuracy.

⚠️ Use with Caution

Accumulation rate predictions should be used cautiously (58% accuracy). Consider additional validation.

📊 Future Improvements
